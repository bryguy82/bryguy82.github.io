Annual Preston Day

By Mary Poppins

This weekend will be a great time to get outdoors. The temperatures will be in the high 70s over the weekend which means it's a great time to head to Preston's famous town center. Not only that, it's our annual barbeque in rememberance of those who founded this town 120 years ago.

A local family, the Nelsons, have generations that have been living here and they plan to continue the trend. The oldest grandson of Jacob Nelson, Michael, will be in charge of this year's events.

Food and a good time will be had for all. The food will be provided, but it is requested that you donate non-perishible food for the event's food drive.
