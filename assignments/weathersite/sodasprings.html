Hot Springs in Idaho!

By Wayne Chandler

Southern Idaho is a great place to find relaxing hot springs. It's in a general hot spring area and close to Old Faithful in Yellowstone. Come and let your worries and stress melt away and relax in one of these natural hot-tubs. The tourist locations are open all year long and ready to give you an unforgettable experience

The health benefits for your skin and your soul are definitely worth the trouble to come to experience them for yourself.

As a word of warning, some of the water temperatures can get dangerously hot to swim in and some springs release toxins, so it's best to check a guide before heading out there. For a complete guide to hot springs in Idaho, click here.
