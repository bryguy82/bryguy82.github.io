Central Park Fun

By John Andrews

Fish haven has a wonderful and elegant central park. It resembles the amazing Central Park in New York. Bring your family to enjoy fall this year as the leaves turn into beautiful fall colors rich with reds and oranges.

Venders of all types will be selling from various food carts and there will be clowns, balloons, dunk tanks, and other simple fair activities. There is no cost to get in, but there are areas where you can purchase tickets.

All those who bring donations for our food bank will automatically be placed into our drawing for an ipad. The events start on Saturday morning at 9 AM and go until 2 PM.
